## Scale Projection (3 Years)

### Year 1: Proof of Concept
- 1 pilot college, 600 students
- Revenue: ₹0 (free pilot to prove model)
- Impact: ₹5.34L in measurable savings demonstrated
- Goal: Documented case study, 2nd college signed

### Year 2: First Revenue
- 6 colleges onboarded (SaaS model)
- Pricing: ₹5,000/month per college
- Revenue: ₹3.6L/year
- Infrastructure cost: ₹72,000/year (Firebase Blaze × 6 projects)
- **Gross margin: 80%**

### Year 3: Scaled Operations
- 25 colleges onboarded
- Revenue: ₹15L/year (₹5,000 × 25 × 12)
- With per-transaction fee: +₹4L/year
- **Total Year 3 Revenue: ₹19L/year**
- Team: 2 developers + 1 ops/support
